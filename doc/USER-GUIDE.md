## Quick Start

### First Visit

1. Open your browser and visit Rainbow BBS
2. Browse popular posts and forums on the homepage
3. No login required to view public content

### Site Structure

- **Top Navigation**: Home, Search, Notifications, Messages, Profile
- **Forum Tabs**: Switch between different discussion areas
- **Post List**: Posts sorted by time or popularity
- **Right Sidebar**: Check-in, Popular Posts, Active Users

---

## Registration &amp; Login

### Register an Account

1. Click the "Register" button in the top right corner
2. Fill in registration information:
   - Username: 3-20 characters
   - Email: For password recovery
   - Password: 6-20 characters
3. Click "Register" to complete
4. System automatically awards "Newcomer" badge

### Login

1. Click the "Login" button in the top right corner
2. Enter username and password
3. Click "Login"

### Logout

1. Click your avatar in the top right corner
2. Select "Logout"

## Browsing &amp; Searching

### Browse Forums

1. Click forum tabs at the top to switch between discussion areas
2. Available forums:
   - All: Show posts from all forums
   - Technology Exchange: Technical discussions
   - Q&amp;A: Ask for help
   - Industry News: News and information
   - Resource Sharing: Share resources
   - Job Hunting: Recruitment and job search
   - Chit-Chat: Casual topics
   - Site Management: Site discussions

### View Post Details

1. Click any post card to enter the detail page
2. You can view:
   - Post content (Markdown supported)
   - Images (click to enlarge)
   - Polls (if any)
   - Comment list
   - Like, Favorite, Share, Report buttons

### Search Function

1. Click the "Search" button at the top
2. Enter keywords to search posts
3. Supports searching by title and content

## Posting &amp; Commenting

### Create a New Post

1. Click the "Post" button at the top
2. Fill in post information:
   - **Title**: Post title (required)
   - **Forum**: Select which forum to post in (required)
   - **Content**: Edit with Markdown editor
   - **Tags**: Add up to 3 topic tags (optional)
   - **Poll**: Add a poll (optional)

#### Using the Markdown Editor

- Supports GFM syntax
- Supports code highlighting
- Supports mathematical formulas
- Supports drag-and-drop/paste image upload
- Supports video upload (50MB limit)

#### Inserting Images

Three ways to insert images:

1. **Drag &amp; Drop**: Drag images directly into the editor
2. **Paste**: Copy and paste images into the editor
3. **Button Upload**: Click "Insert Image" button (small screen only)

#### Creating a Poll

1. Click "Add Poll"
2. Enter poll title
3. Add poll options (2-10 options)
4. Select poll type (single/multiple choice)
5. Set deadline (optional)
6. Click "OK"

### Post a Comment

1. Find the comment box at the bottom of the post detail page
2. Enter comment content
3. Click "Post Comment"

#### Reply to a Comment

1. Click the "Reply" button below a comment
2. Enter reply content
3. Click "Post Reply"

#### @Mention Feature

- Enter `@username` in comments to mention that user
- Mentioned users will receive notifications

## Social Interaction

### Likes

- **Like a post**: Click the "Like" button on the post detail page
- **Like a comment**: Click the "Like" button below a comment
- You will receive notifications when liked

### Favorites

1. Click the "Favorite" button on the post detail page
2. View in "Personal Center" - "My Favorites"

### Follow Users

1. Click a user's avatar or username to go to their profile
2. Click the "Follow" button
3. After following, you can view their latest posts in "Follow Feed"
4. Followee receives 1 reputation point reward

### Unfollow

1. Go to the profile of a followed user
2. Click the "Following" button to unfollow

### Private Messages

1. Click the "Messages" button at the top
2. Click "New Message" or select an existing conversation
3. Enter message content and send
4. Unread messages have a red indicator

### Notifications

1. Click the "Notifications" button at the top
2. View notification list, includes four types:
   - 🔴 Like notifications (red)
   - 🔵 Comment notifications (blue)
   - 🟢 Follow notifications (green)
   - 🟡 Badge notifications (yellow)

## Personal Center

### Access Personal Center

1. Click your avatar in the top right corner
2. Select "Personal Center"

### Profile

#### View Profile

- Avatar
- Nickname
- Signature
- Personal homepage link
- Statistics: Posts, Comments, Following, Followers

#### Edit Profile

1. Click the "Edit Profile" button
2. Modify nickname, signature, personal homepage link
3. Click "Save"

### My Achievements

#### Check-in

1. Click "Check-in" on the right sidebar of the homepage
2. Daily check-in earns 10 reputation points
3. Consecutive check-ins earn an extra 5 reputation points

#### Badges

- View earned badges
- View requirements for unearned badges

## FAQ

### Account Questions

**Q: What if I forgot my password?**
A: Currently you need to contact an admin to reset your password. Email recovery will be added later.

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Usernames cannot be changed after registration, please choose carefully.

**Q: How to change my avatar?**
A: Currently using default avatars. Avatar upload will be added later.

### Posting Questions

**Q: Are there any posting restrictions?**
A:
- Post interval: At least 60 seconds
- Daily post limit: Maximum 10 posts per day
- New users (registered within 7 days): Maximum 3 posts per day

**Q: Can I edit my post after publishing?**
A: Yes, click "Edit" in "My Posts".

**Q: Can I delete my post?**
A: Yes, click "Delete" in "My Posts". Deleted posts cannot be recovered.

### Image Upload Questions

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: Common formats like JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP are supported.

**Q: Is there a file size limit for images?**
A: It's recommended that each image does not exceed 10MB.

**Q: Can't see the image upload button on small screens?**
A: Click the "Insert Image" button above the editor.

### Poll Questions

**Q: Can I change my vote?**
A: Votes cannot be changed, please choose carefully.

**Q: Can I still vote after the poll deadline?**
A: You can't vote after the deadline, but you can view the results.

**Q: Can I see who voted?**
A: No, voting is anonymous.

### Anti-Spam Questions

**Q: Why was my post hidden?**
A: Possible reasons:
- Reported more than 3 times
- Content quality check failed
- Reputation score too low

**Q: How to improve my reputation score?**
A:
- Publish high-quality content
- Earn likes and best comments
- Avoid being reported

**Q: What if I'm banned?**
A: Automatically unbanned after the ban period ends. Contact admin if you disagree.

### Other Questions

**Q: How to report violating content?**
A: Click the "Report" button on a post or comment, select a reason and submit.

**Q: How to contact an admin?**
A: Post in the "Site Management" forum, or send a private message to an admin.

**Q: How to suggest features or report bugs?**
A: Welcome to post in the "Site Management" forum.

## Community Guidelines

### Posting Guidelines

1. Post in the appropriate forum
2. Use clear and specific titles
3. Content should be authentic and useful
4. Comply with laws and regulations
5. Respect others' rights

### Prohibited Behavior

1. Publishing illegal content
2. Publishing advertisements or spam
3. Malicious spamming or flooding
4. Personal attacks or insults
5. Plagiarizing others' content
6. Malicious reporting

### Violation Handling

- Warning
- Content deletion
- Temporary ban
- Permanent account ban
